Sessions

Skate Like a Girl

Commonwealth works closely with Skate Like A Girl PDX, a non-profit dedicated to teaching women and young girls how to skate and provide a fun, comfortable environment to earn those first skateboard bruises. Skate Like A Girl conducts regular skate clinics at Commonwealth on the first and second Sunday of each month from 9:30 AM - 1:00 PM, as well as occasional ladies-only skate sessions, outdoor events and camps.

Skate Like A Girl handles their own clinic registrations and all proceeds benefit their non-profit. Information and Registration can be found on their Skate Clinics page.

Kids Only

Commonwealth has a kids-only session for children 14 and under on Saturday mornings from 10AM - 12:00 PM. This is a good opportunity for younger novice kids to skate Commonwealth without feeling intimidated by some of the older skaters.

Old Man Nights

Every Monday and Wednesday nights from 8PM - 10PM, Commonwealth hosts a 21+ session for the older shredders. And of course ladies are always welcome!
