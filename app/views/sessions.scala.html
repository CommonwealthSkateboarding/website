Our general sessions are free skates open to anyone of any age or ability. Everyone who skates must sign a waiver, and skaters under 18 must have theirs signed by a legal guardian. Minors are also required to wear helmets.

Any and all gear necessary is available to purchase in the shop.

Just Kids

Saturdays

10AM–12PM

$7

Our kids-only sessions are for anyone 14 and under. They take place each Saturday morning from 10AM–12:00PM. This is a good opportunity for younger kids and novices to skate Commonwealth without feeling intimidated by some of the older or more advanced skaters.

Parents or legal guardians must be present to sign a waiver for all first-time skaters, but you are not obligated to stick around.

Helmets are mandatory and available for purchase in the shop, along with any other gear you might need.

Skate Like a Girl Sunday Clinics

Sundays*

9:30AM–1PM

Free**

Commonwealth works closely with Skate Like A Girl PDX, a non-profit dedicated to teaching women and young girls how to skate while providing a fun & comfortable environment to earn those first bruises.

*On the first and second Sunday of each month, Skate Like A Girl conducts skate clinics at Commonwealth. They come in three varieties:

  • Beginners Ages 7 & Under (9:30AM–11AM)
  • Beginners Ages 7 & Up (11:30AM–1PM)
  • Intermediate Youth & All-Levels Adults (10:30AM–12PM)

**Registration is free, but a $10 donation is suggested and goes directly back into the program. Please refer to their Sunday Clinics page to see when the next scheduled / available sessions are.
