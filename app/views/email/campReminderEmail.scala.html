Campers are expected to bring:

Packed Lunch / Lunch Money
We'll be making a short stop for lunch each day, but these stops are chosen for efficiency & ability to quickly accommodate 13 ravenous children, not necessarily health or dietary restrictions. When in doubt, pack a lunch!

Water, water, water.
Skateboarding is a very physically demanding sport and can be especially harsh outdoors under the sun. While we keep backups available, it's important your camper has their own water(s) each day as well. If you or your camper forget, we sell cold bottles at the shop for $1.

Sunblock
Never underestimate the power of ultraviolet rays, even on cloudy days! Please send your camper with their own method of sunblock, preferrably with a layer already applied each morning. There's a 7-Eleven conveniently located across the street in the event you need to stock up.

Helmet & Gear
Pads are optional — and a good idea — but helmets are required. We have helmets in stock ranging from $42-$55, and complete pad sets for $32.
